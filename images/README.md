# Institute Logos

Add the following logo files to this directory:

## Professional Experience Logos

1. **unimi-logo.png** - University of Milan logo
   - Used for 3 positions (PhD Student, Teaching Assistant, Researcher)
   - Recommended size: 400x300px or similar aspect ratio
   - Format: PNG with transparency

2. **waseda-logo.png** - Waseda University logo
   - Used for Visiting Research Fellow position
   - Recommended size: 400x300px or similar aspect ratio
   - Format: PNG with transparency

## Logo Requirements

- Both logos should be transparent PNGs (no white/colored background)
- Height will be set to 60px (automatically scales to maintain aspect ratio)
- Maximum width will be 150px
- Logos are centered above the job title in the timeline

## Where to Find Official Logos

- **Waseda University**: https://www.waseda.jp/top/about/brand/
- **University of Milan (Unimi)**: https://www.unimi.it/en/

You can also search "[Institution Name] official logo" or check the university's press/media kit page.
